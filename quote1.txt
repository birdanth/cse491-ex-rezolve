come to the woods, for here is rest. there is no repose like that of the green
deep woods. here grow the wallflower and the violet. the squirrel will come and
sit upon your knee, the logcock will wake you in the morning.
sleep in forgetfulness of all ill. of all the upness accessible to mortals,
there is no upness comparable to the mountains.
my mother drew a distinction between achievement and success. she said that
'achievement is the knowledge that you have studied and worked hard and done
the best that is in you. success is being praised by others, and that's nice,
too, but not as important or satisfying. always aim for achievzment and forget
about success.'
